# X-Carve

## What This Class Covers

 * Easel
 * 2D Designs

## What We Won't Cover

 * 3D Designs (Ask Shane for help with this)

## Getting Started

 1. Clamp sacrificial board
 1. Clamp work piece
 1. Turn on the computer. Credentials are on the wall left of the machine.
 1. Log in to your inventables account.
